Little Learners Academy Nursery Program

(3-4 years old)

Designed to inspire your little one to explore, create, and interact, the nursery curriculum at Little Learners Academy is a well-structured program delivered through theme-based learning. Teachers guide and nurture curiosity among children, enabling them to discover and learn about the environment, themselves and the world around them.

Fine Motor Skills Development

The ability to hold a spoon or crayon and exercising control over the tripod grip comes from the enhancement of fine motor skills. Activities and games such as picking, sorting, free-hand drawing, paper crushing, etc. involving the use of small muscles, develop the ability to advance fine motor skills.

Gross Motor Skills Development

From catching and kicking a ball to yoga and exercise indoors, children are given the freedom and space to explore their physical abilities to the fullest, developing balance, control over their limbs and independence in movement. This in turn helps build self-confidence, strength and encourages muscle development and growth.

Personal, Socio-Emotional Skill Development

Expressing wants & needs, building conversations, being sensitive to the emotions of peers, forming friendships and playing with others are all signs of PSE skill development. The Nursery program at Little Learners Academy provides varied experiences to stimulate confidence, compassion and collaboration among children, coupled with experiences and interactions to support constructive learning.

Cognitive Skills Development

The ability to learn, acquire knowledge, imitate, respond to words, identify items and make sense of things, stems from cognitive development. Activities ranging from sorting, matching, identifying patterns and creating make-believe stories lay the foundation for early cognitive development.
